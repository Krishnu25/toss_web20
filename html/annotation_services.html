Annotation Services

TOSS provides data collection, image, video, LiDAR, and NLP annotation services, delivering high-quality datasets to enhance AI training and model performance.

We Provide Diverse Types of Annotation Services.

Data Collection & Creation

TOSS streamlines AI data collection with automation and human verification. Our 1M+ AI experts provide high-quality text, image, audio, video, and geo-local data for training models. We handle data enrichment, summarization, and multilingual dataset creation—so you can focus on AI development.

Image Annotation

TOSS specializes in high-quality image annotation for computer vision, combining human expertise and AI-assisted labeling. We support tasks like classification, segmentation, and object detection, ensuring precise annotations tailored to your project’s needs.

Video Annotation

TOSS provides high-quality video annotation for training computer vision models. By labeling objects frame by frame, we create accurate ground truth datasets for applications like self-driving cars, medical AI, and geospatial technology.

LiDAR Annotation

TOSS provides high-quality LiDAR annotation for 360-degree vision, enhancing autonomous vehicle models. Our ML-integrated approach ensures fast, accurate semantic and instance segmentation.

Natural Language Processing

TOSS specializes in NLP, enabling seamless human-machine interaction by training AI to understand and process language accurately. Our team extracts key entities and intents, ensuring precise and efficient AI performance.
